About Us

We are The Word of God Deliverance Vineyard Church, committed to worship, community support, and spiritual growth through weekly services, prayer meetings, and outreach.

Our Mission

To lead people into a deeper relationship with Christ by sharing the gospel and serving every member with love and compassion.

Our Vision

To build a flourishing spiritual family where everyone is empowered, restored, and equipped to live a victorious life.

Our Values

Faith, worship, service, unity, and practical discipleship that honours God and blesses the community.
